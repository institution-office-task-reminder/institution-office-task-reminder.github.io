Step-by-Step

From Installation to Full Institutional Use

Follow these simple steps to deploy Institution Office Task Reminder in your institution.

1

Download & Install

Download the lightweight Windows office software installer and launch it. No complex servers, databases, or tech-heavy setup required—deploy a secure office system to any terminal in less than two minutes.

💻 Platform: Windows 10 or later · 200 MB disk space · 4 GB RAM minimum
2

Automatic Trial Starts

On first run, a 15-day free trial begins automatically. No license file is needed for evaluation. You gain full access to all features immediately so your institution can properly assess the software before any purchase decision.

3

Enter Your Tasks

Begin adding tasks with custom categories, urgency levels, and deadlines, acting as the ultimate to do list for your institution. Attach official documents instantly using the new "Browse..." tool which auto-uploads to your secure Google Drive. Compose details in flawless Native Bangla Unicode (Avro/Bijoy) and delegate duties to multiple staff members simultaneously.

4

Assign Roles & Branding

Enforce strict accountability with distinct user levels (Admin, Editor, Viewer). Personalize the system in the Branding Settings by uploading your official Institution Logo and customizing the app's headers to reflect your school or college identity.

5

Track & Monitor

Use the dashboard, calendar view, and task list to monitor deadlines, overdue items, and daily activities. The dashboard provides at-a-glance status so your team always knows what requires immediate attention.

6

Cloud & Automation Setup

Activate Google Apps Script automation with a single click to trigger daily Telegram reminders and automated system cleanup. The offline-first sync ensures that every team member's computer stays perfectly coordinated in real time.

7

Upgrade to Full License

After your 15-day trial, contact us for a signed commercial license to continue full institutional access. Import the license file through the software's About & License section — activation is immediate.
