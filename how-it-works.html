Simple steps to get started with institutional task management

Step-by-Step

From Installation to Full Institutional Use

Follow these simple steps to deploy Institution Office Task Reminder in your institution.

1

Download & Install

Download the software installer from the download page and run it on your Windows computer. No complex configuration is required — installation takes just a few minutes and the application is ready to run immediately.

💻 Platform: Windows 10 or later · 200 MB disk space · 4 GB RAM minimum
2

Automatic Trial Starts

On first run, a 15-day free trial begins automatically. No license file is needed for evaluation. You gain full access to all features immediately so your institution can properly assess the software before any purchase decision.

3

Enter Your Tasks

Start adding institutional tasks with categories, deadlines, priority levels, and notes. You can organize tasks under categories such as Academic Activities, Institutional Tasks, Government Tasks, Financial Tasks, and Exam Tasks.

4

Assign Roles

Set up user roles — Admin, Editor, or Viewer — based on your institutional staffing structure. Admins have full control, Editors can add and modify tasks, and Viewers have read-only access, keeping your data safe and structured.

5

Track & Monitor

Use the dashboard, calendar view, and task list to monitor deadlines, overdue items, and daily activities. The dashboard provides at-a-glance status so your team always knows what requires immediate attention.

6

Sync & Collaborate

Multiple authorized computers can work with synchronized institutional data. This allows your team to stay coordinated without requiring constant internet — the offline-first design ensures productivity in any environment.

7

Upgrade to Full License

After your 15-day trial, contact us for a signed commercial license to continue full institutional access. Import the license file through the software's About & License section — activation is immediate.
