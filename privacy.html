Privacy Policy

How we handle information and respect your privacy

Introduction

At Institution Office Task Reminder, we value the trust you place in our software. This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect through our website and communications, and more importantly, what we do not collect from your institutional environment.

Information Collection

We may collect information through the following channels:

  • • Contact Forms: When you reach out for a quote or license, we collect your name, email address, and institutional details to process your request.
  • • Direct Communication: Information shared via Email, WhatsApp, or phone for support or licensing purposes.

Software Privacy (Desktop App)

Our software is a standalone desktop application. We do not track your institutional tasks, data, or files on our servers. Your data remains on your local machines or institutional infrastructure as configured by your IT department.

Security & Trust

We follow strict security practices to protect your information:

  • • We will never ask you to provide private encryption keys or sensitive license signing keys.
  • • License files are generated securely and delivered directly to authorized institutional contacts.

Third-Party Services

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. Standard web server logs may be kept by GitHub as part of their hosting service. We may also use external links to reliable services for communication (e.g., WhatsApp).
